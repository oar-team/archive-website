Kameleon recipes

During the 2009 edition of Google Summer of Code, a tool to generate appliances have been created: Kameleon.

We currently have some basic recipes to build debian appliances but we need more of them, for other distributions and more complex.

The goal of this project is thus to continue the work initiated last year and to create more recipes.
